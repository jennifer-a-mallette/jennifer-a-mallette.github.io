Jennifer A. Mallette

Machine Learning Engineer

Jennifer (Jenny) Mallette is a Machine Learning Engineer for IBM's Unified Messaging team. She graduated with her Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Cornell University in 2016, and received her M.Eng. from Cornell in 2017. For the last 5 years at IBM, she has leveraged chat transcript data to create chatbots, provide chat-summary analytics, and build tools to assist representatives during live chat. She has presented this work to multiple conferences, and co-wrote over 20 patents based on the technology.
